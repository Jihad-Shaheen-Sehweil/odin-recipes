Kebab

Description

Kebab, kabob, or kebap or kabab is roasted meat that originates from the Middle East and has been popularised by Iranian cuisine & Turkish cuisine. Many variants of the category are popular around the world, including the skewered shish kebab and the doner kebab with bread.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Marinade
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix the marinate ingredients together well in a large bowl
  2. Add the chicken thighs and marinade for at least 30 minutes or even better, overnight
  3. Thread onto skewers
  4. Cook for about 20 minutes until golden on the outside and cooked through
  5. enjoy
